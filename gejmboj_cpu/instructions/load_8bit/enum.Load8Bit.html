8 Bit load instructions.

LD(SingleRegister, SingleRegister)

Loads data from register r2 into r1.

§

LD_FROM_HL(SingleRegister)

Loads data pointed to by HL into r.

§

LD_TO_HL(SingleRegister)

Loads data in r into location pointed to by HL.

§

LD_N(SingleRegister, u8)

Loads operand into register r.

LD_A_FROM_HL_DEC()

LD_A_FROM_HL_INC()

LD_A_TO_HL_INC()
